PayPal is a leading online payment platform that connects people and businesses worldwide. They offer secure, convenient, and fast payment solutions, making it easy to send and receive money.

I designed a personalized onboarding flow for PayPal's new users. By crafting compelling push notification content, I aimed to motivate users to return to the app and initiate their first purchase. I developed a series of five notifications, each strategically timed to guide users through their initial experience. These notifications highlighted key features, offered personalized recommendations, and provided gentle nudges towards making a purchase. This strategy led to a 20% increase in new users completing their first purchase, demonstrating the effectiveness of my content in driving engagement and conversion.

Content Design

Main challenge: Balancing Engagement and Avoiding Overwhelm.

Key proposed actions:

  • Dynamic Content: Using data points to create personalized messages that resonate with individual users.

  • Contextual Triggers: Sending notifications at the right time, based on user activity or specific events.

  • A/B Testing: Experimenting with different notification variations to identify the most effective approach.
