Amazon Prime Video is a streaming service with a wide selection of movies, TV shows, and Amazon Originals.

I designed a captivating chatbot experience to promote a new Amazon Original Show. The goal was to immerse users in the world of the show and encourage them to engage with the content. I developed a dialog flow that incorporated a variety of multimedia elements, including images, audio, and videos featuring the show's actors. To enhance the user experience, I created content for multiple characters, allowing users to interact with different personalities within the chatbot.

Conversation Design Project Management AI

Main challenge: Interactivity and Storytelling

Key proposed actions:

  • Create a Strong Narrative: Develop a compelling storyline that captures the user's interest and provides a clear purpose for the chatbot interaction.

  • Design Engaging Questions: Craft questions that encourage users to participate and feel invested in the story.

  • Use Multimedia Elements Effectively: Incorporate images, audio, and videos strategically to enhance the storytelling experience.
